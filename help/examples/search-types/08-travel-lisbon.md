# Lisbon Trip Planning — February 2025

## Flights
- **Outbound**: Feb 14, GRU → LIS, TAP TP092, departs 22:15
- **Return**: Feb 22, LIS → GRU, TAP TP091, departs 10:30

## Accommodation
Airbnb in Alfama neighborhood — close to Castelo de São Jorge, walking distance to everything. Host: Pedro. Check-in after 3pm.

## Must visit
- [ ] Pastéis de Belém (the original pastel de nata!)
- [ ] Torre de Belém + Mosteiro dos Jerónimos
- [ ] Time Out Market for lunch
- [ ] Tram 28 through the old neighborhoods
- [ ] LX Factory for artsy shops and cafes
- [ ] Day trip to Sintra (Palácio da Pena)
- [ ] Sunset at Miradouro da Graça

## Food & Drink
- Bacalhau à Brás (codfish dish)
- Francesinha if we do a day trip to Porto
- Ginjinha — sour cherry liqueur, try it at A Ginjinha near Rossio
- Bifana — pork sandwich, simple but incredible

## Budget
- Flights: R$4,200 (already booked)
- Accommodation: €85/night × 8 = €680
- Food + activities: ~€100/day × 8 = €800
- Total estimate: ~R$12,000
