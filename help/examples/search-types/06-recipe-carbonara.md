# Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients (serves 2)
- 200g spaghetti
- 150g guanciale (or pancetta if unavailable)
- 3 egg yolks + 1 whole egg
- 80g Pecorino Romano, finely grated
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt for pasta water

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
2. Cut guanciale into small strips or cubes
3. Cook guanciale in a cold pan over medium heat until crispy (8-10 min)
4. While guanciale cooks, mix eggs + pecorino + pepper in a bowl
5. Cook spaghetti 1 minute less than package directions
6. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining
7. Add drained pasta to the guanciale pan (heat OFF)
8. Pour egg mixture over pasta and toss vigorously
9. Add pasta water a splash at a time until creamy
10. Serve immediately with extra pecorino and pepper

## Notes
- **Never** add cream. Real carbonara gets its creaminess from eggs + cheese + pasta water.
- The residual heat cooks the eggs gently. If the pan is too hot, you get scrambled eggs.
- Guanciale is cured pork cheek — it has a different flavor than pancetta or bacon.
